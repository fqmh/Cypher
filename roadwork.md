# Roadwork

Automated roadwork training. Cypher walks a precise, pre‑mapped route to the training spot, buys what it needs, activates the station, and repeats — tuned to your character's movement speed so the path lands correctly.

---

## Quick start

1. Open the **Roadwork** tab.
2. Click **Pick Window** and select your game window.
3. Choose your **Location** and **Type**.
4. **Enter your in‑game speed %** (this is important — see below).
5. Press **Start Macro**. Press **Stop** (or **F6**) to end.

---

## Settings

- **Location** — which route to run: **Minamoto** or **Senkaimon**. Each has its own mapped path.
- **Type** — train **Speed** or **Stamina** (changes which option the macro selects).
- **In‑game Speed %** — **enter YOUR own in‑game speed buff %** (from talents, clan, gems, etc. — note that being muscly or fat applies a 10% debuff). The route timings are calibrated for a reference speed, and Cypher scales every movement to match your speed so you arrive at the right spots.
- **Cycles** — how many times to repeat the route per run.

> **Why the speed field matters:** the macro moves by holding keys for exact durations. If the speed value doesn't match your character, you'll walk too far or not far enough and the route drifts. Set it accurately for your account.

---

## What it handles for you

- **Walks the route** with precise key‑hold timings, scaled to your speed.
- **Buys the training item** by finding the buy prompt on screen and clicking it.
- **Picks Speed vs Stamina** based on your **Type** choice.
- **Auto‑eats food** when hungry between cycles.
- **Auto‑leave on combat** — if enabled in Settings, it pulls out when combat is detected.

---

## Tips

- If the character keeps missing the spot, your **In‑game Speed %** is almost certainly off — re‑check your real buffs/debuffs and update it.
- As always, the window must be **800 × 600**.

**Emergency stop:** **F6**.
