# Shadow Boxing

A simple, solo training grind. Cypher holds a movement key and clicks continuously to shadow box, periodically re‑equipping your training item and re‑buying it so the grind never stalls.

---

## Quick start

1. Open the **Shadow Boxing** tab.
2. Click **Pick Window** and select your game window.
3. Press **Start**. Press **Stop** (or **F6**) to end.

---

## What it does

- **Continuous training** — holds the movement key and clicks to keep shadow boxing.
- **Periodic re‑equip** — every short cycle it re‑equips the training item and your weapon so you don't drop out of the activity.
- **Re‑buys the item** on a slower cycle so you don't run out.
- **Auto‑eats food** when hungry (checked on the re‑buy cycle).
- **Stamina aware** — rests when stamina is low (releasing the movement key while it waits) and resumes automatically.
- **Auto‑leave on combat** — if enabled in Settings, it pulls out when combat is detected.

---

## Tips

- Keep food and your training item stocked in your hotbar so the re‑equip/re‑buy and auto‑eat steps have what they need — see [Inventory Slotter](inventory-slotter.md).
- Run the **Monitor** alongside for disconnect/combat alerts while you're away.

**Emergency stop:** **F6**.
