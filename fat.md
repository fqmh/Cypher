# Fat

A focused "fat" training grind. Cypher rapidly cycles equips and clicks to train, and watches your training slot so it knows when something's wrong.

---

## Quick start

1. Open the **Fat Macro** tab.
2. Click **Pick Window** and select your game window.
3. Press **Start**. Press **Stop** (or **F6**) to end.

---

## What it does

- **Rapid training cycle** — repeatedly equips the training item and clicks to train as fast as the activity allows.
- **Slot watching** — keeps an eye on the training item in your first hotbar slot. If that item disappears and stays gone (for example, you've run out of money or food), the macro assumes the grind can't continue and **leaves the game** so you're not stuck doing nothing.

---

## Tips

- Make sure your training item is in the **first hotbar slot** and that you have enough currency/food to keep buying it — otherwise the slot‑watch will (correctly) bail out.
- Run the **Monitor** alongside for alerts while AFK.

**Emergency stop:** **F6**.
