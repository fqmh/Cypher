# Quality‑of‑Life Features

Beyond the macros, Cypher is packed with "set it and forget it" features that keep your accounts healthy, safe, and informed. Most live in **Settings**; here's what each one is for.

---

## Anti‑AFK
Keeps Roblox from kicking you for inactivity by sending a harmless key on a timer to your game windows.

- **Anti‑AFK** — turn it on.
- **Anti‑AFK Key** — which key to tap (default **Space**).
- **Auto Return Focus** — return focus to your previous window after the anti‑AFK tap, so it doesn't interrupt what you're doing.
- **Test Anti‑AFK** — fire a test key to all game windows right now to confirm it's working.

---

## Auto‑Leave
Pulls an account out of the game automatically when something dangerous happens (combat, or a raid base). Ideal for protecting AFK alts.

- **Leave game after Combat** — the main switch.
- **Leave Mode** — **Instant** (leave immediately) or **Normal** (wait for combat to end, then leave).
- **Combat Evasion** — try to dodge/survive instead of just bailing.
- **Leave on Raid Base** — also leave when a raid base appears.
- **Monitor Off After Leave** — stop the Monitor once it has leaved an account.
- **Shutdown PC on Alert** — shut the PC down after an alert/leave (for overnight farming).

---

## Auto‑Return (auto‑rejoin)
The counterpart to Auto‑Leave: when an account **disconnects** or **stops training**, Cypher waits for things to settle and **rejoins automatically** through a Roblox deep link — and can hop to a quieter server.

- **Auto‑Return Macroing** — the main switch.
- It respects a **cooldown** between attempts and a **give‑up timeout** so it won't retry forever.
- It can **prefer low‑population servers** when rejoining.

> Auto‑Return is what lets a grind survive a disconnect overnight without you touching it.

---

## Auto‑Wear
Re‑equips your training gear automatically when training resumes after an interruption, so you never come back to an un‑equipped character.

---

## FPS control
Manage Roblox's frame rate to save resources while AFK.

- **FPS Cap** — turn capping on.
- **FPS Value** — the cap (default 30).
- **Uncap Focused** — remove the cap on whichever window you're actually looking at, so the active account stays smooth while the rest are capped.

---

## Game Overlay (HUD)
A small on‑screen overlay that shows live status (raid cooldowns, macro status) right over your game.

- **Enable Overlay** — show it.
- **Lock Position (Click‑through)** — lock it in place and let clicks pass through it so it never gets in your way.
- **Show Setsuna / Show Yakuza (Raid 1) / Show SWAT (Raid 2)** — choose which cooldowns appear.
- **Test Overlay HUD** — preview it.

---

## Periodic Stat Check
Have Cypher press a key on a schedule to pop your stats/menu (e.g. to keep a check‑in going).

- **Enable**, choose a **Key** (1–7, R, T, Y), and an **Interval (mins)**.

---

## Periodic Screenshot & Combat Capture
- **Periodic Screenshot** — save a screenshot every few minutes (set the interval) so you have a visual history of your AFK session.
- **Combat Screenshot** — automatically capture the screen when combat is detected, so you can see what attacked you.

---

## Medal Clip (F8)
- **F8 Medal Clip** — when enabled, Cypher can trigger a Medal.tv clip with **F8** (and uses it around key moments), handy for saving highlights.

---

## Discord Webhook alerts
Every important event can ping a Discord channel.

- **Webhook URL** — your channel's webhook.
- **Discord Mention ID** — your user/role ID so alerts can ping you.
- **Delete messages after** — auto‑clean alert messages after a chosen time, or **Keep Pings** to keep them.

See [Getting Started](getting-started.md#6-recommended-connect-discord) for how to create a webhook, and the [Settings Reference](settings.md) for every option.
